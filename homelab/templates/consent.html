🤝 Before you identify anyone

An unnamed voice is just an anonymous cluster — not tied to any real person. The moment you name one, you're linking that voice to a real identity, so:

  • You confirm you have that person's permission to associate their voice with their identity.
  • You're responsible for using the device lawfully where you are (recording laws vary by place).
  • Anyone can be set to don't-profile or deleted (voiceprint + all data) at any time.
